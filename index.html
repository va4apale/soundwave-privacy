SoundWave Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Overview

SoundWave is an offline music player. It does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

SoundWave does not collect any data. The app accesses audio files stored locally on your device solely for the purpose of playback. No data ever leaves your device.

Permissions

SoundWave requests access to your audio files only to play music. No other data is accessed.

Third Parties

SoundWave does not use any analytics, advertising, tracking services, or third-party SDKs that collect data.

Internet

SoundWave does not require or use an internet connection.
